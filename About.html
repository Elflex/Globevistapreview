Our Story

From a routing desk to a full-service travel design studio.

Every itinerary is handled by planners who've flown the routes themselves. No templates, no commission-driven upsells — just honest recommendations we'd actually book.

Globevista Travels was born out of frustration. The founder — a frequent flyer with a packed schedule — was tired of juggling five tabs, three booking sites, and a dozen confirmation emails just to get from Lagos to Dubai.

So they built a better way. A single routing desk that handled everything: flights, hotels, transfers, and activities. One conversation, one itinerary, one point of contact.

Today, that routing desk has grown into a full-service travel design studio. But the core principle hasn't changed: we plan every trip like we're going ourselves.

We don't use algorithms to recommend hotels we've never seen. We don't push commissions over convenience. Every destination we feature, every route we route, every stay we suggest — we've vetted it, visited it, or planned it for someone we know.

Whether you're traveling for business, family, or adventure, we take the logistics off your plate so you can focus on the experience. That's the Globevista difference.
